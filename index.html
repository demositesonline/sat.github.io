EXPLORING THE QUALITY WAYS THROUGH DESIGN.

SAT is an independent specialized elevator & escalator support service provider for both New Equip-ment and Service Business. We provide mechanical and electrical installation including testing & com-missioning support for new installations and modernizations. We are experts in managing complex repairs and providing 24x7 technical assistance for troubleshooting of all type of elevators and escalators.

We provide cost effective solutions ensuring safety, quality and reliability. Our highly flexible service options offer freedom and peace of mind to OEM. Our mission is to assist you in installing or maintain-ing a safe and reliable elevator with the minimum cost possible. We guarantee to meet your needs and expectations, letting you to have peace of mind .
